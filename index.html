Typography System

Elegant serif and clean sans-serif combinations with hierarchy examples

Primary Typeface - Playfair Display

Elegant Design

Usage: Headlines, hero titles, section headers

Weights: 400, 500, 600, 700

Character: Sophisticated, luxury, timeless

Secondary Typeface - Inter

Clean and readable body text for all content areas

Usage: Body text, captions, navigation, buttons

Weights: 300, 400, 500, 600

Character: Modern, clean, highly legible

Typography Hierarchy

H1 - Main Headlines (Playfair Display, 48px)

H2 - Section Titles (Playfair Display, 36px)

H3 - Subsection Headers (Playfair Display, 28px)

H4 - Card Titles (Inter, 20px, Semibold)

Body Text - This is the primary text used for content areas, descriptions, and general information. (Inter, 16px, Regular)

Small Text - Used for captions, metadata, and secondary information. (Inter, 14px, Regular)

Competitor Analysis

Analysis of top interior design websites and portfolio presentations

High-End Interior Design Sites

Common Patterns Observed:

  • Minimal navigation with elegant typography
  • Full-width hero images with overlay text
  • Neutral color palettes with selective accent colors
  • Grid-based portfolio layouts with hover effects
  • Emphasis on white space and clean lines

Portfolio Presentation Styles

Grid Layout

Equal-sized thumbnails in responsive grid

Pros: Clean, organized, easy to scan
Cons: Less visual hierarchy

Masonry Layout

Variable-sized images in flowing layout

Pros: Dynamic, showcases image variety
Cons: More complex to implement

Featured + Grid

Large featured project with smaller grid items

Pros: Hierarchy, highlights best work
Cons: Requires careful project selection

Brand Personality References

Visual references for different brand personality expressions

Luxury & Sophistication

Colors: Deep neutrals, gold accents, monochromatic schemes
Typography: Elegant serifs, generous spacing, refined letterforms
Layout: Asymmetrical balance, ample white space, premium feel
Imagery: High-contrast, dramatic lighting, architectural details

Modern & Minimalist

Colors: Clean whites, soft grays, selective color pops
Typography: Sans-serif, clean lines, optimal readability
Layout: Grid-based, systematic, functional hierarchy
Imagery: Clean lines, natural light, uncluttered spaces

Warm & Inviting

Colors: Earth tones, warm neutrals, organic color schemes
Typography: Friendly serifs, approachable sans-serifs, comfortable sizing
Layout: Organic flow, curved elements, comfortable spacing
Imagery: Soft lighting, natural textures, lived-in spaces

Bold & Dramatic

Colors: High contrast, saturated accents, dramatic pairings
Typography: Strong weights, impactful sizing, statement headers
Layout: Dynamic angles, overlapping elements, visual tension
Imagery: Strong contrasts, bold compositions, statement pieces
